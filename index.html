About Us

JMYB is a small, not-for-profit community band, playing mainly symphonic band music and original compositions. We aim to give amateur wind, brass and percussion players the opportunity to rehearse and perform in a friendly and supportive environment. We rehearse in the City of Whitehorse area on Saturday afternoons. Members of all ages are welcome, with a preferred minimum skill level of AMEB Grade 5 or equivalent. Please see our About page for more information on the band. If you're interested in joining us, visit our Contact page.
